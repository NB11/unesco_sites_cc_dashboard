About This Map

Data Sources

  • Climate Data: Climate Impact Lab - Annual Average Temperature (tas_annual) projections
  • Administrative Boundaries: World Administrative Boundaries GeoJSON
  • UNESCO Heritage Sites: UNESCO World Heritage Centre
  • Map Tiles: MapLibre GL JS

Climate & Damage Projections

Climate projections use CMIP6 (O'Neill et al., 2016) with SSP2-4.5, showing annual average temperature changes across time periods and percentiles (5th, 50th, 95th). Damage projections use CMIP5 (Taylor et al., 2012), with plans to update to CMIP6.
